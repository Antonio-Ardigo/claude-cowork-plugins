# Waterproofing Methods

## Under-Slab (External)
- System: HDPE sheet membrane (1.5mm) or bituminous sheet
- Surface prep: blinding must be smooth, dry, projection-free
- Application: roll out, heat-weld laps (150mm overlap)
- Protection: 50mm sand blinding over membrane before rebar

## Internal Walls
- System: Crystalline cementitious (Xypex/Penetron/Sika)
- Alternative: Epoxy coating (2-coat) for chemical resistance
- Surface prep: grit blast to ICRI CSP-3, remove laitance
- Application: 2 coats brush/spray, 24hr between, 2mm total DFT
- Curing: keep damp 48 hours after final coat

## Internal Floor
- Same as walls (crystalline or epoxy)
- Apply after hydrostatic test passes (confirm no structural cracks)
- Alternative: integral waterproofing admixture in mix

## External Walls
- System: Bituminous membrane (torch-on, 2-layer)
- Primer: bituminous, apply to dry concrete
- Laps: 150mm side, 200mm end
- Protection: 6mm polystyrene or geotextile before backfill

## Waterstops
- PVC dumbbell: 250mm wide, cast into construction joints
- Installation: wire-tied to rebar, centered on joint
- Hydrophilic strip: adhesive-bonded at construction joints
- Joint sealant: PU gun-applied, 25mm x 12mm bead
