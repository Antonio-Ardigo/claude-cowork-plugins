# Measurement Rules

## Concrete
- Net volume (no deductions for rebar displacement)
- Separate by grade and element type
- Wastage: 2.5% slabs, 5% walls/columns

## Reinforcement
- Net cutting length + lap lengths (40d standard, 50d full tension)
- Wastage: 5% straight bars, 8% complex shapes
- Rebar intensity check: 80-120 kg/m3 concrete (water tanks)

## Formwork
- Contact area (face of concrete)
- Walls: both faces (inside + outside)
- Slabs: soffit area (horizontal projection)
- Columns: all 4 sides; beams: 3 sides (soffit + 2 sides)
- Kicker: perimeter x 150mm height x 2 faces

## Earthworks
- Neat line + working space (1.0m each side)
- Excavation: in-situ volume (no bulking factor)
- Backfill: compacted volume (not loose)
- Disposal: excavated minus backfill reuse

## Waterproofing
- Applied area (developed surface)
- Laps included in rates, not area measurement

## Joints & Waterstops
- Linear meters at each construction joint
- Include corners and intersections

## Rounding
- Quantities: nearest whole number
- Rates: 2 decimal places
- Amounts: nearest dollar
