# Board Meeting Simulator

Simulate a board of directors meeting to stress-test proposals before presenting them for real.

## How It Works

Run `/board-meeting` and describe your proposal. Six directors — Chair, CEO, CFO, COO, General Counsel, and an Independent Director — will debate it, challenge it, and vote on it. You get formal meeting minutes with a resolution, conditions, and action items.

## Command

| Command | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `/board-meeting` | Submit a proposal and get a full board deliberation |

## The Board

| Role | Lens |
|------|------|
| Chair | Governance & process |
| CEO | Strategy & vision |
| CFO | Financial rigor |
| COO | Execution & operations |
| General Counsel | Legal & compliance |
| Independent Director | Shareholder & skeptic |

## Example

```
/board-meeting

We propose acquiring a 30-person AI startup for $45M to accelerate
our machine learning capabilities. The target has $2M ARR, strong
technical team, and two pending patents. Integration would take 6-9 months.
```

The board will debate the acquisition from every angle — strategic fit, valuation, integration risk, IP exposure, shareholder value — and deliver a vote with conditions.
