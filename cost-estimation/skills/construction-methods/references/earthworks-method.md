# Earthworks Method

## Procedure
1. **Setting out**: Survey grid, benchmarks, excavation limits
2. **Topsoil strip**: 150-300mm, stockpile for landscaping
3. **Bulk excavation**: To formation (-1.0m to -1.5m below FGL)
4. **Working space**: 1.0m minimum each side of structure
5. **Formation trimming**: Final 200mm by hand/grader to exact level
6. **Proof rolling**: 10t roller, 4 passes minimum
7. **Disposal/reuse**: Suitable material stockpiled for backfill
8. **Dewatering**: If water table within 2m of formation

## Quantities (100,000 m3 tank example)
- Excavation area: ~135m x 135m (incl. working space) = 18,225 m2
- Depth: 1.0m average
- Volume: ~18,225 m3 + overdig
- Backfill: ~4,000-6,000 m3 (perimeter)

## Backfill
- Material: selected granular fill, free from organics
- Layers: 300mm maximum loose thickness
- Compaction: 95% MDD (modified Proctor)
- No backfill against walls until roof slab complete (prevent lateral pressure on unbraced wall)
