# Concrete Placement Procedures

## Base Slab
- Pour strips: max 30m x 15m = 450 m2 per pour
- Sequence: alternate strips (checkerboard) to control shrinkage
- Rate: 120 m3/day with concrete pump
- Vibration: internal poker, max 50mm from form face
- Finishing: power float for exposed, rough for bonded screed

## Walls
- Lift height: 3.25m per lift (2 lifts for 6.5m)
- Pour rate: max 2m/hour rise (formwork pressure)
- Drop: max 1.5m free fall (use tremie for higher)
- Cycle: form (2d) + rebar (3d) + pour (1d) + cure (1d) = 7 days/lift
- Kicker: 150mm starter, cast with base slab

## Curing
- Water-retaining: 7-day continuous wet cure
- Non-retaining: 3-day minimum
- Methods: ponding, sprinklers, curing membrane, wet hessian

## Hot Weather (Gulf/Saudi)
- Max placement temp: 35C (SASO/EN 206)
- Max internal temp: 65C (SASO)
- Cooling: chilled water, ice, liquid nitrogen
- Aggregates: shade stockpiles, spray with water
- Night pours: Jun-Sep mandatory for large elements
- Start curing immediately after finishing

## Testing
- Slump: every 50 m3 or every truck (target 150+/-25mm)
- Cubes: 6 per 50 m3 (3x 7-day, 3x 28-day)
- 28-day strength: min fck + 1.48s
- Temperature monitoring: thermocouples in mass pours >500mm
