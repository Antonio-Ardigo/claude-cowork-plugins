# Key Equipment Cost Curves & Typical Delivery Times

## How to Use These Curves

These are **budget-level equipment cost benchmarks** for Class 4-5 estimates when vendor quotes are not yet available. They represent FOB (free on board) purchase prices for major equipment in standard materials and configurations.

Adjustments required:
- **Material upgrade**: Duplex SS, super duplex, titanium, Inconel --> multiply by 1.5 - 3.0x
- **Design pressure/temperature**: Higher ratings increase cost
- **Special features**: Explosion-proof, SIL-rated, API vs ASME
- **Market conditions**: Tight vendor backlog can add 10-30%
- **Currency/escalation**: Benchmarks in mid-2020s USD

## Notes

- All prices are FOB (free on board), excluding transport, installation, and commissioning.
- Delivery times are from PO placement to ex-works / ready for shipment.
- Actual delivery depends on vendor backlog -- add 2-6 months in tight market conditions.
- Material upgrades (duplex, super duplex, titanium, Inconel) can multiply base price by 1.5x - 3.0x.
- API-610/ASME/PED compliance is assumed for the prices shown. Non-code equipment may be cheaper.
- For desalination-specific equipment, prices assume seawater-grade materials (super duplex, GRP, titanium) as standard.
- Always request budget quotes from vendors as early as possible to validate these benchmarks.
