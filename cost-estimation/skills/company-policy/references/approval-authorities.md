# Approval Authorities

## Estimate Approval
| Value | Approver | Review Required |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| < $1M | Senior Estimator | Peer review |
| $1M - $10M | Chief Estimator | Senior + peer review |
| $10M - $50M | Commercial Director | Chief + independent review |
| > $50M | Board / Executive Committee | Full audit + external benchmarking |

## Tender Markup
- Standard markup (per policy): Chief Estimator
- Reduced markup (competitive): Commercial Director
- Zero/negative margin: Board approval required

## Client Discount
- Up to 5%: Project Director
- 5-15%: Commercial Director
- Above 15%: Board
