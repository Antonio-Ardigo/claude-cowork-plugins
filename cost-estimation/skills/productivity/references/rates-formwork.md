# Formwork Erection & Striking Rates

## Base Rates (per carpenter, temperate climate)
| Element | Erect (m2/day) | Strike (m2/day) | Reuse Cycles |
|---------|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Wall panels (steel) | 12 | 24 | 8-12 |
| Slab soffit (table forms) | 15 | 30 | 6-10 |
| Beam sides | 10 | 20 | 6-8 |
| Column forms (steel adj.) | 8 | 16 | 10-15 |
| Kicker | 20 | 40 | 4-6 |

## Gang Outputs
| Element | Gang Size | Erect (m2/day) | Strike (m2/day) |
|---------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| Wall panels | 10 carpenters | 50 | 100 |
| Slab soffit | 8 carpenters | 40 | 80 |

## Striking Times (at 20C)
| Element | Min Strike Time | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Wall panels | 12-16 hours | If concrete > 10 MPa |
| Slab soffit (unpropped) | 7 days | Full design load |
| Slab soffit (backpropped) | 3 days | With 2-level propping |
| Beam sides | 12-16 hours | Self-supporting |
| Beam soffit | 14 days | High shear/moment |
| Column | 12-16 hours | Self-supporting quickly |

## Formwork Pressure
- Max: 60 kN/m2 (2m pour height, 6m/hr rise, 2.4 SG concrete)
- Design pressure = gamma_concrete x min(H_pour, rate x set_time)
