# RC Water Tank -- Construction Sequence

## 18-Step Method
1. Site preparation & setting out
2. Excavation to formation (-1.0m to -1.5m below FGL)
3. Formation compaction & proof rolling
4. Blinding concrete (75mm C12/15)
5. Waterproofing membrane (under slab)
6. Base slab rebar + formwork + concrete (pour strips max 30m x 15m)
7. Kicker construction (150mm starter, PVC waterstop in joint)
8. Wall Lift 1 (3.25m): rebar, formwork, concrete, cure
9. Wall Lift 2 (3.25m): rebar, formwork, concrete, cure
10. Column construction (400mm x 400mm, single pour)
11. Roof beam construction (300mm x 600mm)
12. Roof slab construction (200mm C30/37)
13. Internal waterproofing (walls + floor, 2-coat cementitious)
14. External waterproofing (bituminous torch-on)
15. Backfill compaction (300mm layers, vibratory roller)
16. M&E installations (pipes, valves, ladders, ventilation)
17. Testing (hydrostatic: fill, hold 7 days, measure)
18. Commissioning & handover

## Multi-Cell Strategy (2x2)
- Base slab: monolithic across all cells (sequential pour strips)
- Walls: cell-by-cell, max 2 cells simultaneously
- Stagger cell starts by 1-2 weeks
- Roof: follows wall completion per cell

## Joint Types
- Construction: at pour strip boundaries (with waterstop)
- Movement: between cells if span > 30m
- Expansion: between independent structural units
