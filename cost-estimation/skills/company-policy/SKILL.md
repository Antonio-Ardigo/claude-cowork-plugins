# Company Policy -- Estimating Rules & Standards

## Purpose
Company policy defines HOW to present and approve estimates. These rules always override generic industry practice. They cover procedures, deliverables, approvals, and quality checks.

## Key Policy Areas
1. **Estimating procedures**: Step-by-step workflow from scope to submission
2. **Risk allowances**: Mandated contingency ranges by project phase
3. **Approval authorities**: Who can approve what value of estimate
4. **Reporting format**: Mandatory deliverable list (18-sheet XLSX + DOCX)
5. **QC requirements**: Pre-submission checklist (10-point audit)

## Policy Override Principle
When company policy conflicts with generic industry practice:
- Company policy ALWAYS wins
- Flag any deviation from policy in the audit report
- Document the reason for any approved deviation
