# Risk Assessment -- Uncertainty & Contingency

## Purpose
Risk assessment identifies and quantifies cost and schedule risks. It feeds into contingency calculation and sensitivity analysis. Top-5 cost items are varied +/-20% to produce tornado diagrams.

## Key Outputs
- Risk register (10+ risks with probability, impact, mitigation)
- Sensitivity analysis (tornado diagram on top-5 cost drivers)
- Contingency recommendation based on risk profile
- Break-even analysis for key assumptions

## Top-5 Cost Drivers (Typical RC Tank)
1. Reinforcement steel (25-35% of direct cost)
2. Concrete (20-25%)
3. Preliminaries/site staff (10-15%)
4. Formwork (8-12%)
5. Waterproofing (5-8%)
