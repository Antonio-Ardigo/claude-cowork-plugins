# Reinforcement Fixing Rates

## Base Rates (per fixer, temperate climate)
| Bar Dia | Fix Rate (kg/day) | Cut+Bend Shop | Wastage |
|---------|------------------|---------------|---------|
| 12mm | 200 | 500 kg/day | 5% |
| 16mm | 250 | 600 kg/day | 5% |
| 20mm | 300 | 700 kg/day | 5% |
| 25mm | 350 | 800 kg/day | 7% |
| 32mm | 400 | 900 kg/day | 8% |

## Gang Outputs (adjusted for element type)
| Element | Gang Size | Output (t/day) | Notes |
|---------|-----------|----------------|-------|
| Base slab | 14 fixers | 2.0 | Heavy bar 25-32mm |
| Walls | 12 fixers | 1.6 | Vertical fixing, difficult |
| Columns/beams | 8 fixers | 1.2 | Complex shapes |
| Roof slab | 10 fixers | 2.4 | Lighter mesh, flat |

## Lap Lengths
- Standard (tension): 40 x bar diameter
- Full tension: 50 x bar diameter
- Compression: 30 x bar diameter

## Multiple Gangs
- Large tanks: up to 4-5 rebar gangs concurrent
- Each gang needs own chargehand + supervisor
- Coordination: stagger by zone/cell to avoid congestion
