## Preliminaries (Egypt-specific)
- Mobilization: $30,000 (local workforce, no camp needed)

## Markups
- Contingency: 12% (higher due to currency risk, supply chain)
- Escalation: 8% (EGP volatility, inflation ~25-30% in EGP terms)
- OH&P: 10% (lower overheads, competitive market)
- **Total markup: ~30%**

## Validated Example
- 100,000 m3 RC rectangular tank: $7.5M total (EGP 375M) = $75/m3 stored
- 45% below international, 35% below Saudi

## Egypt-Specific Considerations
- **Currency risk**: EGP has depreciated significantly since 2022. All USD rates assume Q4 2025 exchange rate. Include escalation clause in contracts.
- **Import dependency**: Specialty items (waterstops, membranes, valves) often imported -- price in USD, add 10-15% for customs/clearance.
- **Productivity factor**: 0.85x (climate: hot, Ramadan impact, working culture differences)
- **Quality variability**: Concrete and rebar quality variable -- budget for additional testing (add 0.5% to direct cost)
