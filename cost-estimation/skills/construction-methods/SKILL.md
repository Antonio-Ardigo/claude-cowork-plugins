# Construction Methods -- How Things Are Built

## Purpose
Construction methods define HOW things are built -- step-by-step sequences, procedures, and technical requirements. They are independent of cost (prices) and speed (productivity). Methods inform both productivity rates and cost build-ups.

## Key Method Areas
1. **RC tank overall sequence**: 18-step process from site prep to handover
2. **Earthworks**: Excavation, formation, compaction, dewatering
3. **Concrete placement**: Pour strips, lift heights, vibration, curing, hot weather
4. **Formwork systems**: Steel panels, table forms, striking times
5. **Waterproofing**: Internal coating, external membrane, waterstops
6. **Dewatering**: Sump pumping, wellpoints, deep wells
7. **Testing & commissioning**: Hydrostatic test, acceptance criteria, disinfection
