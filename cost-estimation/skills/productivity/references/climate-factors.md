# Climate Productivity Factors

## Adjustment Factors by Zone
| Climate Zone | Factor | Key Constraints |
|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| Temperate (baseline) | 1.00 | Standard 8-hr day, 5-day week |
| Hot arid (Gulf/Saudi) | 0.80 | Heat ban 1200-1500, split shift, night pours Jun-Sep |
| Tropical (SE Asia) | 0.85 | Monsoon delays, high humidity |
| Cold (Nordic/Canada) | 0.85 | Winter heating, shorter daylight |
| Extreme cold (<-20C) | 0.70 | Heated enclosures, accelerated curing |

## Saudi/Gulf Specific
- **Summer schedule**: 0500-1100 + 1600-2000 split shift (Jun-Sep)
- **Mandatory heat ban**: 1200-1500 hrs daily (Labor Law)
- **Night concrete pours**: mandatory Jun-Sep for temperature control
- **SASO limits**: max concrete internal 65C, max placement 35C
- **Chilled water/ice**: required in summer concrete mixes
- **Rebar fixing**: -15% to -25% vs temperate (ScienceDirect 2022)

## Working Patterns
| Region | Work Week | Daily Hours | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Gulf (summer) | 6 days | 8 hrs (split) | 0500-1100 + 1600-2000 |
| Gulf (winter) | 6 days | 10 hrs | 0600-1600 continuous |
| Europe | 5 days | 8 hrs | Standard 0800-1700 |
| SE Asia | 6-7 days | 8-10 hrs | Monsoon shutdowns |
| North America | 5 days | 8-10 hrs | Winter shutdowns in cold zones |

## Ramadan
- Embedded in Gulf 0.80x factor
- Additional -5% to -10% during Ramadan month
- Shorter working hours, lower energy levels
- Duration: ~30 days, shifts annually
