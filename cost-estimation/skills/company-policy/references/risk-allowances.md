# Risk Allowances by Project Phase

| Phase | Contingency | Typical | Accuracy |
|-------|------------|---------|----------|
| Concept/Feasibility | 20-30% | 25% | +/-50% |
| Pre-FEED | 15-20% | 18% | +/-30% |
| FEED | 10-15% | 12% | +/-20% |
| Detailed Design | 7-10% | 8% | +/-15% |
| Post-Tender | 3-5% | 4% | +/-10% |

## Additional Risk Allowances
| Risk | Add to Contingency |
|------|-------------------|
| Unknown ground (no SI) | +3-5% |
| Remote/difficult access | +5-10% |
| Compressed schedule | +5-8% |
| New technology/first-of-kind | +10-15% |
| Unstable currency (floating FX) | +2-5% on escalation |
