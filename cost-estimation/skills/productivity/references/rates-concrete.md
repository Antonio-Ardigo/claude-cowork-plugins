# Concrete Placement Rates

## Base Rates (temperate climate, 1.00x factor)
| Element | Rate (m3/day) | Gang Size | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-----------|-------|
| Blinding C12/15 | 80 | 8 laborers | Simple unreinforced pour |
| Base slab C35/45 | 120 | 14 laborers | Pump-placed, vibrated |
| Walls C35/45 | 80 | 12 laborers | 2m lift height, vibrated |
| Columns | 25 | 6 laborers | Small pours, high rebar |
| Roof beams | 30 | 8 laborers | Elevated, confined forms |
| Roof slab C30/37 | 160 | 18 laborers | Large area, pump-placed |

## Placement Constraints
- Pour strip max: 30m x 15m (base slab)
- Wall lift height: 3.25m (2 lifts for 6.5m wall)
- Concrete drop: max 1.5m free fall
- Pour rate: max 2m/hour rise (formwork pressure)
- Pump line: max 100m horizontal, 30m vertical

## Curing
- Water-retaining: 7-day continuous wet cure
- Non-retaining: 3-day minimum
- Method: ponding, sprinklers, or curing membrane

## Hot Weather (Gulf)
- Max placement temp: 35C (SASO/EN 206)
- Max internal temp: 65C (SASO)
- Cooling: chilled water, ice, liquid nitrogen
- Night pours: mandatory Jun-Sep for large elements
