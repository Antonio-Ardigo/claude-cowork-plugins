# Crew Compositions

## Standard Crews
| Crew Type | Composition | Output/day | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Concrete (slab) | 1 foreman + 14 laborers + 1 pump op | 120 m3 | Includes vibrators |
| Concrete (wall) | 1 foreman + 12 laborers + 1 pump op | 80 m3 | Lift-by-lift |
| Concrete (column) | 1 foreman + 6 laborers | 25 m3 | Small pours |
| Rebar (base slab) | 1 chargehand + 14 fixers | 2.0 t | Heavy bar 25-32mm |
| Rebar (walls) | 1 chargehand + 12 fixers | 1.6 t | Vertical fixing |
| Rebar (columns) | 1 chargehand + 8 fixers | 1.2 t | Complex bends |
| Rebar (roof) | 1 chargehand + 10 fixers | 2.4 t | Lighter mesh |
| Formwork (walls) | 1 foreman + 10 carpenters | 50 m2 erect | Steel panels |
| Formwork (soffit) | 1 foreman + 8 carpenters | 40 m2 erect | Table forms |
| Earthworks | 1 foreman + 10 laborers + ops | 500 m3 | Excavator + trucks |
| WP (membrane) | 1 supervisor + 6 applicators | 160-320 m2 | System-dependent |

## Supervision Ratios
- Supervisor-to-labor: 1:10 (general), 1:6 (skilled trades)
- Site engineer covers 2-3 gangs
- Safety officer: 1 per 100 workers

## Overtime & Premiums
- Overtime (first 2 hrs): 1.5x rate
- Overtime (beyond 2 hrs): 2.0x rate
- Night shift premium: 1.25x
- Weekend/holiday: 2.0x

## Concurrent Gangs (Large Tanks)
- Rebar: up to 4-5 gangs, staggered by zone
- Formwork: up to 3 sets rotating between cells
- Concrete: 2 gangs sequential (pump capacity limit)
