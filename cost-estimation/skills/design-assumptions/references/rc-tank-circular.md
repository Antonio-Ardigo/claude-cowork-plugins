# RC Circular Water Tank -- Design Parameters

## Typical Configurations
| Volume (m3) | Diameter | Height | Wall Thickness |
|------------|----------|--------|---------------|
| 1,000 | 15m | 5.5m | 250mm uniform |
| 5,000 | 30m | 6.0m | 300mm uniform |
| 10,000 | 40m | 8.0m | 350-400mm tapered |
| 20,000 | 50m | 10.0m | 400-500mm tapered |
| 50,000 | 70m | 13.0m | 500-700mm tapered |

## Wall Profile
- **Uniform**: constant thickness, suitable for H < 6m
- **Tapered**: thicker at base (max hoop stress), thinner at top
- Taper ratio: typically 2:1 (base:top thickness)

## Roof Options
- **Flat slab**: column-supported, for D < 30m
- **Dome**: self-supporting, for D > 30m, most efficient
- **Column-supported flat**: for any diameter with internal columns

## Prestressing (Large Tanks)
- Circumferential tendons: wire-wound or strand
- Vertical prestressing: post-tensioned tendons in wall
- Design code: EN 1992-3 or ACI 350/AWWA D110

## Joints
- Vertical construction joints: every 30-40 degrees of circumference
- Wall-base joint: monolithic or pinned (with waterstop)
