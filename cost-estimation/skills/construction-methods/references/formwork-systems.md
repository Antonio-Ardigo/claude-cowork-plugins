# Formwork Systems

## Wall Formwork (Steel Panel)
- System: Doka/PERI/Hunnebeck steel panels with climbing brackets
- Panel size: 2.4m x 1.2m standard modules
- Max pour height: 3.5m per lift (3.25m with safety margin)
- Ties: she-bolt system, min 4 per m2
- Alignment: plumb +/-5mm per 3m height (EN 13670)
- Reuse: 8-12 cycles (clean + oil between uses)

## Slab Soffit (Table Forms)
- System: Table forms with drop-head system (rapid strike)
- Span: up to 8m between props
- Backpropping: 1 level below for single-story tanks
- Drop-head: early strike at 3 days, props remain for 28 days
- Reuse: 6-10 cycles

## Column Forms
- System: Steel adjustable 4-piece column forms
- Size: 400mm x 400mm (project-specific)
- Bolted assembly, no ties needed
- Reuse: 10-15 cycles

## Striking Times (at 20C)
| Element | Min Time | Condition |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Wall panels | 12-16 hrs | Concrete >10 MPa |
| Slab soffit (unpropped) | 7 days | Full design load |
| Slab soffit (backpropped) | 3 days | With propping below |
| Beam sides | 12-16 hrs | Self-supporting |
| Beam soffit | 14 days | High shear/moment |
