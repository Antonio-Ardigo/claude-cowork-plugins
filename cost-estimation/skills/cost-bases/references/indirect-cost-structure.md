# Indirect Cost Structure -- Detailed Breakdown Template

## Purpose
This file defines the 10-category detailed breakdown of indirect costs (project preliminaries). The Indirect Costs sheet in the XLSX workbook expands the BOQ Section 1 (Preliminaries) into a fully itemized cost buildup. The total of this sheet MUST equal the Section 1 total in the BOQ (no double-counting).

---

## Category 1: Manning / Staff

Project staff cost buildup by role, monthly rate, and duration.

### Typical Manning for RC Water Tanks

| Role | Qty | Rate Range (Intl $/mo) | Rate Range (Saudi $/mo) | Duration Basis | Notes |
|------|-----|----------------------|------------------------|----------------|-------|
| Project Manager | 1 | $8,000-12,000 | $6,000-10,000 | Full project duration | From mobilization to handover |
| Site Engineer (Civil) | 1-2 | $5,500-7,500 | $4,000-6,500 | Earthworks to commissioning | Core site supervision |
| Site Engineer (M&E) | 0-1 | $5,500-7,500 | $4,000-6,500 | Last 30% of project | Piping and commissioning phase |
| QA/QC Engineer | 1 | $5,000-7,000 | $4,000-6,000 | Blinding to commissioning | Inspection and testing |
| Safety Officer | 1 | $4,500-6,500 | $3,500-5,500 | Full project duration | Mandatory for all sites |
| Surveyor | 1 | $4,000-6,000 | $3,000-5,000 | Setting out to as-built | Intermittent in later phases |
| Quantity Surveyor | 1 | $5,000-7,000 | $4,000-6,000 | Full project duration | Cost control and measurement |
| Site Admin/Document Controller | 1 | $2,500-4,000 | $1,500-3,000 | Full project duration | Record keeping |
| Storekeeper | 1 | $1,500-2,500 | $800-1,500 | Full project duration | Material management |

### Manning Duration Rules
- Full duration roles: PM, Safety, QS, Admin, Storekeeper
- Core construction roles: Site Engineers, QA/QC, Surveyor -- typically 80-90% of total duration
- Late-phase roles: M&E engineer -- typically last 25-35% of project

### Calculation
**Staff cost = SUM(Qty x Rate x Duration in months) for each role**

Typical staff cost as % of direct: 6-10% (International), 4-7% (Saudi/Gulf)
