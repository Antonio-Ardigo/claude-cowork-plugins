# Audit & QA -- Estimate Validation

## Purpose
Audit-QA validates completed estimates against benchmarks and checks for errors. Every estimate should pass a 10-point audit before submission.

## 10-Point Audit Checklist
1. Zero formula errors (#REF!, #DIV/0!, etc.)
2. BOQ totals = Cost Summary totals (exact match)
3. No hardcoded values in formula cells
4. All rates within +/-25% of published market range
5. Quantities match design dimensions (+/-5%)
6. All productivity rates have cited source
7. Markups match company policy for project phase
8. Schedule matches duration calculation (+/-10%)
9. Risk register has minimum 8 risks identified
10. All cost data has source reference with date

## Cost Benchmarks ($/m3 stored, RC rectangular tanks)
| Region | Range |
|--------|-------|
| International | $100-180/m3 |
| Gulf/Saudi | $80-150/m3 |
| Egypt/South Asia | $50-120/m3 |
