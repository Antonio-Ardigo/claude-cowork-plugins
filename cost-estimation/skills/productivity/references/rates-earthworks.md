# Earthworks Productivity Rates

## Base Rates (temperate climate)
| Activity | Rate | Equipment | Gang |
|----------|------|-----------|------|
| Site clearing | 650 m2/day | Bulldozer D6 | 6 workers |
| Bulk excavation (sand) | 500 m3/day | 30t excavator + trucks | 10 workers |
| Bulk excavation (clay) | 300 m3/day | 30t excavator + trucks | 10 workers |
| Bulk excavation (rock) | 100 m3/day | Breaker + excavator | 8 workers |
| Formation trimming | 150 m2/day | Grader | 4 workers |
| Proof rolling | 400 m2/day | Vibratory roller | 3 workers |
| Backfill compaction | 250 m3/day | Roller + loader | 8 workers |
| Soil disposal (20km) | 400 m3/day | 6 trucks | 6 workers |

## Notes
- Rates assume good access, dry conditions
- Sandy soils (Saudi/Gulf): increase excavation rate by 20-30%
- Clay soils: reduce rate by 20-30%, weather-dependent
- Rock: reduce rate by 70%, may need blasting
- Backfill in 300mm layers, 95% MDD compaction
