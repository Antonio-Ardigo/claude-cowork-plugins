## Preliminaries (UAE-specific)
- Mobilization (worker accommodation): $60,000 (camps less remote than Saudi)
- Demobilization: $25,000
- Site offices/welfare (22 mo): $9,000/mo
- Insurance & bonds: $50,000
- HSE (22 mo): $4,500/mo (OSHAD/Musanada standards)

## Markups
- Contingency: 8%
- Escalation: 4% (Expo/infrastructure wave tapering)
- OH&P: 13% (Lower camp costs than Saudi, less Emiratization burden)
- **Total markup: ~25%**
