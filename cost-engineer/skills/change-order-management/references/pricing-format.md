## Tips for Complete Pricing

1. **Always show the calculation** — don't just present lump sums
2. **Reference contract rates** where they exist (labor rates schedule, equipment schedule)
3. **Attach vendor quotes** for materials and subcontracts
4. **Show manhour estimate basis** — reference similar past work, industry norms, or detailed task lists
5. **Separate added scope from deleted scope** — show credits explicitly
6. **Include schedule analysis** — CPM fragment showing impact on critical path
7. **Cross-reference** to RFIs, drawings, and correspondence that justify the change
